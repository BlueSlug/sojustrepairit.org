This site is under co-design by youth and partners

Learn more about co-design
The goal of this project is to co-design and develop a platform of multiple online resources and hubs that enable youth movements and social justice initiatives to:
  • share, repurpose and reuse resources;
  • provide a community-curated set of toolkits, applications, tools and examples;
  • offer a safe supportive space for participant youth with learning differences
The platform will be openly available to any group or individual hosting youth movements, youth action events and social justice movements
Supported by the Oak Foundation and led by the Inclusive Design Research Centre
